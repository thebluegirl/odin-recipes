Classic Waffles Recipe

Description

This is a simple recipe for classic waffles.

This takes about 30 minutes in total prep time and serves 5-6 people.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour (2 cups)
  • Salt (1 tsp)
  • Baking powder (4 tsp)
  • White sugar (2 tbsp)
  • Eggs (2)
  • Milk (1.5 cups)
  • Butter (0.75 cups)
  • Vanilla extract (1 tsp)

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix together flour, salt, baking powder and sugar; set aside.
    • Preheat waffle iron to desired temperature.
  2. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs. Stir in the milk, butter and vanilla. Pour the milk mixture into the flour mixture; beat until blended.
  3. Ladle the batter into a preheated waffle iron. Cook the waffles until golden and crisp. Serve immediately.
